Outstanding Core Features of The Product

User-Friendly

TimeO's wathces are user-friendly. They are easy to use and come with a guidebook to help you understand about it's features. You can wear them anywhere, for parties, casuals, sports etc. Can withstand high temperatures and waterproof upto 50m.

High Security

Our watches are highly secured and can only be unlocked by the owner. The watch can be connected with the phone and a password as well as fingerprint can be applied. More such features are included which makes TimeO standout from others.

Unlimited Updates

The watch undergoes updates every 2-3 months. These updates are to help the customers use the watch more effectively and track their daily progress. The previous updates can be uninstalled manually via phone or the watch. These updates mainly include weather reports, gps tracking system etc.

Unlimited Features

The watch comes with various features such as GPS Tracker, Live chat features for WhatsApp, Weather Reports, Notification Alert, Health tracker and many more to help you ease out your tasks throughout the day.

Features Available

Notification Alert

Get all the notifications on your watch and stay updated with your friends and the happenings around the world.

Live Chat

Connect your social handles with TimeO to be able to text your loved ones. The watch allows you to call and text. The amazing battery life allows the watch to stay active for upto 4 hours.

Heart Beat Tracker

The heart beat tracker feature allows you to track your heart rate during workouts. The tracker is on everytime and can also help you count your pulse. The tracker also counts the calories burnt, the number of steps walked and has more amazing featues.

GPS tracker

With in-built GPS Tracker fucntions, you can reach the places safely without looking into your phone again and again. It helps you stay updated with the life traffic, shortest route possible and the estimated time to reach your destination.

Wifi Facility

You can now connect your TimeO watch with the wifi nearby and access all the features with ease. Can easily connect to wifi's upto a range of 10m.

Weather Updates

With an inbuilt Weather API, the watch gives you regular updates about the weather conditions. It can also give an estimate weather report for the next 3 days.
